Getting Started

Welcome to the AI Chat Assistant documentation. Here you'll find everything you need to know about using our application effectively.

Using the Chat

Our AI assistant can help you with a variety of tasks:

  • Answer questions on various topics
  • Provide recommendations and suggestions
  • Help with research and information gathering
  • Assist with writing and content creation

Voice Commands

You can interact with the assistant using voice commands:

  • Click the microphone icon to start voice input
  • Speak clearly and naturally
  • The assistant will respond to your voice queries

Image Uploads

You can upload images for the assistant to analyze:

  • Click the upload icon to select images
  • Use the camera icon to take photos directly
  • The assistant can describe images and answer questions about them
